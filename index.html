ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Stelios, owner, developer, and publisher of Legend Knight Games, based in Athens, Greece. With several years of experience working in Unity and C#, I’m passionate about turning ideas into immersive gaming experiences. My love for video games started in childhood, and today, I’m dedicated to bringing that passion to life through game development. Driven by clear goals and creativity, I’m excited to continue growing and creating within this dynamic industry.
